About Me

I was born in Silver Spring, Maryland on December 25, 1992(Yes, I'm a Christmas baby!) from two Jamaican parents. I lived there until 1997 when my family moved to West Palm Beach, Florida. In 2008, I got accepted in the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme for Suncoast High School in Riviera Beach, Florida. I was able to pass the program and graduate with the IB diploma and from there I got accepted to the University of South Florida in Tampa to the pre-engineering program in the Summer of 2011. After a year in the program, I dediced to switch my major to a dual major in Business and Social Science and I graduated with the Bachelors in December 2015. Next month, I moved back to West Palm Beach and got a job as a case worker in a hospital. While working there, I orginally planned to apply to graduate school for a masters in social work. However, working there for about a year made me question if I want to purse that career until I dedcided that I want to pursue another career. While working as a case worker, I became interested in programming by doing in for fun. I later decided to pursue a career as a software developer.Orginally, I was planning to go for a masters in Computer Science until I found the post-baccalaureate program for Oregon State University, so here I am today!
